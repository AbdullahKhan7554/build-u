Our Story

More Than a Gym.
A Transformation Hub.

Built-U Gym was founded on a single conviction: that every individual deserves access to world-class training in an environment that demands excellence. Located at the heart of Gulberg III on MM Alam Road, we have engineered a space where ambition meets infrastructure.

Our facility features premium equipment, fully air-conditioned floors, and a team of certified trainers who treat your goals as their own. Whether you're beginning your fitness journey or pushing competitive limits — this is where transformation is built.

A Message from Our CEO

Premium Fitness, Within Your Reach

Dear Lahore Community,

When we laid the foundation of Built U Gym in Gulberg, Lahore, on October 15, 2021, we did so with one clear vision: Fitness is a right, not a luxury.

Our mission has always been to provide premium facilities, world-class equipment, and an inspiring environment without making fitness unaffordable.

Whether you're a student, a working professional, or someone beginning their fitness journey, Built U Gym is designed for you.
